## Welcome to LeadDesk 5

LeadDesk 5 (LD5) is the production enrichment pipeline for dental practice data.

- **Batch Processing** - Upload CSV files and process hundreds of records in parallel
- **Real-time Tracking** - Monitor job progress with live updates
- **Automated Workflows** - n8n-powered workers handle scraping and data aggregation  
- **Multi-Format Export** - Download as CSV, Excel, or JSON
